About Us

Who are we?

Our names are Antony and Ali. We are two Grade 8 students at SAIL who have a passion for helping refugees.

The story behind Refugee Pal

Did you know that Refugee Pal started off as a science project? In January of 2023, we were tasked with coming up with an innovation for the upcoming science fair at our school. Many ideas came, and were promptly discarded when they proved to be not good enough.

However, we ended up settling on Refugee Pal. We both wanted to help refugees who had been forced to leave their homes because of violence. When arriving in Canada, refugees face many difficulties getting the help they need. They lost their social connections, their job certificates, their homes and even their families. Ali came up with an app that would make refugees' lives better. We partnered up, and Refugee Pal was born.
