Connect with any professional you need as a Refugee

As a refugee in british columbia, it is important and necessary that you have the ability to connect and find any professional service you need. Whether it be a settlement worker, doctor, or psychologist. However, language barriers may prevent some individuals from seeking these services, and thats where Refugee Pal comes in. With the connect with professional feature you can navigate your way to a better life in British Columbia as a Refugee.
